Spring 2024

Mechanical Pendulum Clock

Objective: Design and fabricate a pendulum-driven mechanical clock to analyze its timing performance using center of mass, moment of inertia, and oscillation period calculations.
Personal Contributions: Generated hand sketches and CAD models of clock components, fabricated parts using laser cutting and manual machining operations (drilling, reaming, tapping, press fits). Assembled the pendulum mechanism and performed timing analysis using the center of mass and rigid-body pendulum frequency calculations.
Results: Successfully fabricated and tested a pendulum-driven clock mechanism, achieving a 0.6s oscillation period and 7s escapement wheel revolution time.
