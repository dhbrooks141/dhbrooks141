CNC Handheld Projectile Launcher

Personal Contribution: Programmed CNC toolpaths and machined launcher baseplate, brackets, and kinematic front and back from 303 stainless steel and PMMA stock.

Spring 2024

Mechanical Pendulum Clock

Objective: Design and fabricate a pendulum-driven mechanical clock to analyze its timing performance using center of mass, moment of inertia, and oscillation period calculations.
Personal Contributions: Generated hand sketches and CAD models of clock components, fabricated parts using laser cutting and manual machining operations (drilling, reaming, tapping, press fits).
Results: Successfully fabricated and tested a pendulum-driven clock mechanism, achieving a 0.6s oscillation period and 7s escapement wheel revolution time.

Spring 2024

RC Projectile Robot

Objective: Design and fabricate a competition RC robot capable of collecting and delivering projectiles to designated scoring zones during a 60-second head-to-head robotics competition.
Personal Contribution: Designed and fabricated the drivetrain assembly on Fusion 360, integrating the wheel-axle system and traction components, and conducted torque and traction analysis to determine the motor requirements for mobility under payload conditions.
Results: Validated drivetrain performance through torque and traction analysis for a 1.77kg robot, calculating 0.022Nm required torque and measuring 0.292Nm motor torque, confirming sufficient drivetrain capability for robot mobility during competition operation.
